MIT Entrepreneurship Club

Welcome to MIT's Premier Entrepreneurship Club!

We are a community of founders and an interface for students looking to work at startups. We offer a startup curriculum, speaker sessions, and large events for the entire MIT community.

A Community of Founders

At our core, we are over 50 students passionate about tackling the world's toughest challenges with an entrepreneurial mindset. By utilizing MIT resources and strengthening the overall entrepreneurship community on campus, we learn essential skills and build vast networks to help us work on successful ventures early in our professional journeys.

An Interface for Students Looking to Work at Startups

We work to encourage students to pursue internship and full-time startup opportunities. We help top startups recruit MIT students. These carefully selected startups provide immersive work experiences for students to contribute to meaningful projects and receive mentorship.
