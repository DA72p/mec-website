MEC's Mission

Our mission is to make MIT a top entrepreneurial university. We believe that, in our technology-heavy culture and love for innovation, MIT is the perfect university to make this happen. With a growing number of students asking "What do I really want to do?", and increasing dissatisfaction with the common options, MEC offers a creative, disciplined, and uniquely enterprising answer.

We are MIT's largest entrepreneurship club for undergraduates. We host speakers, break down startup pitches, teach entrepreneurial curricula, and much more. Our members are comprised of students who love the startup world, with many having founded or worked at startups!

Getting an idea, convincing customers and VCs, and maintaining a successful company is no easy feat. The journey is circuitous and, perhaps, intimidating. Our goal is to demystify entrepreneurship and make it accessible to all, so that you can thrive in a fast-paced world and unravel the complexity of calling a business yours.
